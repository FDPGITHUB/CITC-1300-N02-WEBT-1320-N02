LAB STUDIOS

"I find immense satisfaction in undertaking straightforward branding projects, and one such memorable endeavor involved crafting a cohesive brand identity. The client sought a color palette exuding trustworthiness and expertise, reflective of their proficiency in storytelling. Additionally, they desired the inclusion of a camera symbol within the logo, aligning with their primary focus on video content creation. Employing a blend of Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop, I meticulously crafted both the branding elements and the accompanying landing page. While the client ultimately opted for a different direction, this project remains a standout experience for me. It compelled me to step outside my comfort zone, fostering a deeper exploration of Adobe Illustrator and igniting a spirit of experimentation that continues to enrich my design practice."
