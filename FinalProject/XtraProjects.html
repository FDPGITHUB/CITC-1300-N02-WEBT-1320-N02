ShoeGameTime Logos

This project was characterized by its simplicity yet meaningful intent: crafting a logo/emblem centered around the initials 'SGT.' The client's vision was clear—to evoke a sense of speed and energy that resonated with the brand's core objective: delivering high-quality shoes promptly and affordably. To meet this vision, I meticulously curated a distinctive highlighter shade of yellow, selecting hex codes and employing color-picking tools to ensure precise alignment with the client's specifications. The result was a visually striking emblem that not only captured the essence of speed and vitality but also stood out with its unique hue, embodying the brand's commitment to excellence and individuality. The client was pleased with both outcomes but leaned towards the latter. Although I deemed both worthy of display as I'll admit I love both logos too much to only showcase one! Haha. This project exemplified the gratifying nature of creative collaboration, where multiple iterations led to equally compelling results, each imbued with its own unique charm and character.
